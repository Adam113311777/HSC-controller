About the Project

FlexiPilot is an innovative, modular controller designed to be fully customizable for your robotics and drone projects. Developed with the needs of developers, IT professionals, and hobbyists in mind, it promises a flexible platform that adapts to a variety of applications.

Currently in early development, we’re working toward version 3—an iteration that will address existing challenges and move us closer to testing and production. We’re here to gauge interest and gather feedback from the community, so your input is crucial.
